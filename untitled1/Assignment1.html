Assignment #1

General Description

This semester I will be creating a website to help a freelance make-up artist showcase her portfolio and upcoming events.
While having the ability to micromanage her time and appointments appropriatly.

Ultimatly her clients will be able to view her work through the gallery page and proceed to booking an appointment through an interactive calender.

Link To Instagram

Structure Of The Website

Each page will contain a banner containing the logo of the company "Kiss And Tell" going along the whole page, along with a drop down menu containing the following possibilities.

Structure

  1. Home
    • Small autobiography
    • Events
    • Links to FaceBook and Instagram page
    • Link to appointment page
  2. Gallery
    • Contains Pictures of all her previous artwork and events
  3. Rates
    • List of different makeup packages and pricing
  4. Contact
    • This page will ask user to set an appointment date
    • Display other contact information

Page Description

Home

The home page will welcome you with a small autobiography of the makeup-artist along with contact information and some pictures of her previous work and certifications.

This page will also contain some information about upcoming events that she will be hosting along with links to her facebook and instagram pages.

If the visitor is interested in setting an appointment there will be a link to the contact page.

Gallery

The gallery page will display images in different categories, for example if the visitor is interested in bridal makeup there will be section allocated strictly to that category.

Rates

This page will display all the information about pricing and the different makeup packages available.

Contact

Here the visitor will be able to find the makeup artists contact information such as email address and phone number along with links to social media pages.

There is also the option to book an appointment through an interactive calender that will ask you for a time and date and will either refuse or accept if the space are available.
